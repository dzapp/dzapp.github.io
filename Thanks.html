My Project

For my Bar Mitvah project I've decided to raise money and awarness to help WIJABA. The money I raise will go towards building a new library in an elementary school in Chihuahua, Mexico, which is intended to open on Memorial Day 2017. The library is going to cost about $15,000 and my goal is to raise about a third of it. For every donation made we will send you a bookmark to remind you how important it is to read, and that you are giving a child in Mexico a special opportunity.
